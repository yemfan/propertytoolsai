## The broker's command center

- **Brokerage-wide pipeline roll-up** — every agent's pipeline visible in one view; no spreadsheet, no Slack ping for status
- **Real-time leaderboard** — speed-to-lead, response rate, appointments-set, closes, by agent. Visible to all agents and ranked. Top producers compete for the top spot; underperformers can't hide.
- **Lead-distribution rules you control** — round-robin, geo, top-producer-priority, opt-in. Set policy once, system enforces it. Agents can't cherry-pick or sandbag.
- **Agent-fitness flags** — automatic alerts when an agent's response time slips, a hot lead goes 24h cold, or an agent's pipeline is shrinking week-over-week. Catch retention risk *before* they leave.
- **Onboarding pipeline** — new agent gets a checklist (license verified, MLS linked, AI templates approved, first lead routed). Track every recruit from offer-letter to first sale.
- **Brokerage compliance posture** — TCPA opt-in audit, supervised AI review queue, communications archive. One audit-ready dashboard, not 50 agents' inboxes.

## What every agent gets (the mechanism that makes it work)

- AI SMS / email / voice nurture in under 5 minutes per lead, in the agent's voice, with TCPA disclosures baked in
- AI CMA generator — agent enters an address, gets an agent-branded comparative market analysis ready to send to a homeowner, in 60 seconds
