## Honest caveats

These models assume:

1. **Existing lead spend stays constant.** ROI accelerates dramatically if you reinvest the savings into more lead volume.
2. **Conservative conversion lifts.** Field data from comparable AI-driven nurture deployments shows 1.5–3pp lifts; we model 0.5pp to stay defensible.
3. **GCI per side of $9,000.** Adjust to your market (LA / SF / NYC are 2–3× this; tertiary markets are 0.5×).
4. **Replacement cost per lost agent of $8,000.** Source: NAR + Inman estimates of recruiting + onboarding + foregone cap fees. Higher-cap brokerages have replacement costs of $15,000+.
5. **20% net brokerage cap.** Adjust to your actual model.
6. **First-year retention lifts are the hardest to capture.** Year 2 retention lift compounds; we don't model it.

We're happy to rebuild this model with your actual numbers in the working session.
